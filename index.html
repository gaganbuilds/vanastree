Vanastree is:

Vanastree is a women-centred collective rooted in the forest home gardens of the Western Ghats of South India. Through seed conservation, ecological learning and community knowledge, the collective supports small-scale food cultures connected to land, biodiversity and memory.

Seed offerings are available under the . Seed Section Please click here for a list of currently available seeds.

Why our work matters

Vanastree shows that the big challenges around food security and social change can have small, dynamic, local answers — working models rooted in the ecosystems people already know best.
