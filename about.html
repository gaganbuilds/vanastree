About vanastree

Vanastree germinated in 2001, having been the seed of a dream for years. We have grown from one person and one village to about 150 persons in 15 villages.

Over the years, the ideas and practices associated with Vanastree have travelled beyond the Malnad through seed sharing, publications and community exchange. The collective continues its core work through its seed savers’ network and the documentation and sharing of ecological knowledge.
