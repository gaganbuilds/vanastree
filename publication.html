Publications

The forest home gardens of the Malnad hold rich traditions of food, seeds and ecological knowledge passed down across generations. Through books, reports and digital publications in Kannada and English, Vanastree has documented and shared these traditions while also creating practical resources on home gardening, biodiversity and ecological living for a wider audience.

ಮಲೆನಾಡ ಕೃಷೋಟ ಕೃಷಿಡಿ (Malenadu Home Garden Handbook)
