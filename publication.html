Publications

The forest home gardens of the Malnad hold rich traditions of food, seeds and ecological knowledge passed down across generations. Through books, reports and digital publications in Kannada and English, Vanastree has documented and shared some of these stories, practices and traditions.

Vanastree: Women of the Forest

This documentary follows the people behind the collective — among them Manorama Joshi, a seed-leader and key organiser; Sunita Rao, Vanastree's founder; and Shyamala Hegde, who works on biodiversity and education in her region. Told through the women themselves, the film traces the collective's challenges and successes as they work to strengthen their communities.

Watch it on Vimeo via Skye Toth Productions, or visit the filmmakers at vanastreefilm.wordpress.com.
