Publications

The forest home gardens of the Malnad hold rich traditions of food, seeds and ecological knowledge passed down across generations. Through books, reports and digital publications in Kannada and English, Vanastree has documented and shared some of these stories, practices and traditions.

BOOKS IN PRINT

ಹಿತ್ತಲ ಸುತ್ತಲೂ ( Hithalu Suthalu )

— ಮಲೆನಾಡ ಕೃಷೋಟ ಕೃಷಿಡಿ

This 248 page richly illustrated handbook brings together forest home gardening wisdom from the women seed savers and gardeners of Malnad — soil to seed, water to wellbeing, medicinal plants, food traditions, and step-by-step guidance for growing food at home in most areas of the Indian sub-continent and beyond. 2026.
