Seeds & How to Order

We produce organic, open-pollinated seeds of traditional varieties — grown by our member farmers in the home gardens and fields of the Malnad region.

Please email completed order forms & payment confirmation to malnadseedsavers@gmail.com. Our seed coordinator will respond with availability and next steps.

Retail outlets

Beyond direct seed orders, a small selection of Vanastree products is also available through partner retail outlets and at our seasonal Malnad Mela festivals. Get in touch for current stockists near you.

Why open-pollinated seeds?

Open-pollinated, traditional varieties can be saved and regrown year after year, keeping farmers independent of outside seed supply and preserving genetic diversity that's been shaped by generations of careful selection in the home garden.
